Script to control live video flow with the following instructions
g - convert to greyscale video .
n - convert to colored video .
s - save image .
v - start recording video .
b - stop recording video .
q or ESC - quit .
p - pause display .
o - continue display .
BONUS -
t - draw rectangle with two left clicks on video .
c - draw circle with one click with constant radius or two clicks variable
radius .
l - draw line with two clicks, and draw as many lines as you want .
i - draw roi of place you want on the screen with two left clicks and
display it in the top left corner of the screen, make roi scalable with +
and - .
r - reset the drawn lines and roi.
